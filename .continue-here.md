## Decisions Made

- **MA examined = per-subject boolean**: Added `ma_examined` field to each subject entry rather than a group-level count. Frontend counts examined subjects per dose group from individual flags.
- **Confidence undefined when n not provided**: When `treatedN`/`controlN` are both null/undefined, `confidence` is `undefined` (not "adequate"). Backward compatible ŌĆö existing callers without n values get no confidence field.
- **Dashed line for low N**: Chosen over reduced opacity or color change. Dashed lines are standard visual convention for uncertainty. Consistent with existing peak-to-terminal dotted connector.
- **`" *"` suffix on verdict label**: Lightweight annotation, standard scientific convention for qualified results.
